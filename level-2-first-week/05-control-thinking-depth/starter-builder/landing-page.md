## Problem Section

You're losing customers and you don't know it yet.

By the time churn shows up in your monthly report, the customer has already left. Their usage dropped three weeks ago. Support tickets went unanswered. The renewal conversation never happened.

Nova Analytics watches the signals your dashboard doesn't. Usage drops, sentiment shifts, engagement gaps — surfaced in real time, routed to the person who can act.

## Features Section

- **Multi-Channel Alerts** — Slack, email, SMS, webhooks. Pick the channel that matches the urgency.
- **Churn Risk Score** — ML-powered score updated hourly. Not a vanity metric — a trigger for action.
- **Segment Builder** — Slice your customer base by plan, usage pattern, lifecycle stage. Build segments in clicks, not SQL.
- **Playbook Automation** — When a signal fires, run the playbook. Assign the CSM. Send the email. Create the ticket. No manual steps.

## Competitive Landscape (internal — not on the page)

- **Mixpanel/Amplitude:** Analytics-first, not action-first. They show you the data. We route it to the person who needs to act.
- **Gainsight:** Enterprise-heavy, $50K+ ACV. We're $299/mo and live in 30 minutes.
- **Intercom:** Messaging-first. Good at talking to customers. Weak at predicting who's about to leave.
- **Homegrown dashboards:** Most teams start here. The problem: dashboards don't page anyone at 2am when the enterprise account stops logging in.

## Voice Guidelines

- Confident but not arrogant
- Specific over vague — numbers, timelines, named tools
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
- Never: "revolutionary," "game-changer," "unleash," "leverage," "cutting-edge"
- Always: concrete scenarios over abstract benefits

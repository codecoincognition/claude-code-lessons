# Nova Analytics — Brand Notes

## Voice
- Friendly, not corporate
- Confident, not salesy
- Simple language — if a 12-year-old can't understand it, rewrite it

## Colors
- Primary: #1a1a2e (dark navy)
- Accent: #e94560 (coral red)
- Background: #f8f8f8 (light gray)

## Words We Use
- "insights" not "analytics"
- "dashboard" not "control panel"
- "alerts" not "notifications"
- "connect" not "integrate"

## Words We Never Use
- "leverage"
- "synergy"
- "best-in-class"
- "enterprise-grade"
- "scalable solution"

## Newsletter Philosophy
- Lead with the human story, not the feature
- One tip that makes readers feel smart
- Short. Founders are busy.
- End with something they can do right now
